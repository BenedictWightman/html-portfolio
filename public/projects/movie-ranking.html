Ben's Ultimate Guide to Film Favourites

My top 3 movies of all time.

Hot Rod

Possibly the most ridiculous movie I have ever watched and yet it remains my favourite.

Captain America: The first Avenger

RIP Cap. Probably the coolest avenger out of the lot and a great film combining the grit of the old wars with the might of modern superheros of the time.

Interstellar

A thought provoking masterpiece. As a young scientist at university, this film expanded my mind further than I had ever thought before.
